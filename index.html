Plot

In this action, adventure, and fantasy movie, Humans have discovered a new planet, Pandora, in the solar system and their main purpose is to harvest a mineral that is very rare and also very valuable. There is a different species of beings known as the Na'Vi and the Humans have created simulators where they remotely control the body of a Na'video person. An ex-marine finds himself controlling one of these Na'Vi beings and gets lost in reality. He decides to turn against his own race in order to protect their planet from destruction.
